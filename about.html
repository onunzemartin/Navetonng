About The Company

Naveton Nigeria Limited, a company registered in Nigeria in 2011 and in technical partnership with Addicon Foods and Beverages GmbH, Hamburg -Germany, has been involved in the procurement and supply of Enzymes and ingredients to the Milling Industry. Our head office is at 223 Ikorodu Road, llupeju while the factory is located at Agiliti - Mile 12. both in Lagos: In a bid to bridge the gap created by the ban on use of bromate in baking, and to assist bakeries solve the twin challenges of lack of appropriate bromate replacers and their availability, NAVEBAKE® Bread Improver was developed. NAVEBAKE® Bread Improver, which is a blend of high performing enzymes with high enzyme activities and oxidants, produced in Nigeria and registered with NAFDAC, has undergone successful baking trials in several 5akeries in Nigeria and bevond. NAVEBAKE® Bread Improver is a product presented in 10g sachets to be used for 100kg of flour each. The second registered pack size is 100 sachets of 7g for 50kg flour each.
